Chrome/Edge extension Stock monitor made by meerSoftware

StockMonitor is a browser extension for Chrome and Edge that allows users to track stock prices in real time.
After installing the extension, users can add their favorite stocks to the watchlist.
The extension automatically fetches updated price information every minute from Yahoo.com.

Users can track up to three stocks for free. To track more than three stocks,
they can upgrade to a subscription of $2 per month, which unlocks unlimited stock tracking.
When a user attempts to add more than three stocks, they are redirected to the subscription checkout page.

The extension is installed directly from the Chrome/Edge Web Store by clicking the “Add” button.
Once installed, the extension runs inside the user’s browser and displays live stock information.

Delivery of Service:
After payment, users instantly receive unlimited tracking access inside the extension.
No manual steps are required — the upgrade is automatically applied to the user’s installed extension.
